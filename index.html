Why most campaigns waste money

Most businesses are not failing because Google Ads cannot work. They are failing because the campaign is loose, the targeting is weak, and the clicks do not match the actual offer.

Bad targeting

Ads reach the wrong users, in the wrong places, with the wrong search intent.

Wasted spend

Budget disappears into clicks that never become real opportunities.

Weak alignment

The search, ad, and landing page do not line up well enough to convert.

No clear focus

The account chases activity instead of quality leads and efficient acquisition.
